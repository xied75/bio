Usage:	   LayoutRefinementUtil.exe [options] <input delta alignment file> <input query file>

LayoutRefinement: Refines alignment layout by taking in consideration indels (insertions and deletions) and rearrangements between two genomes. Requires mate-pair information to resolve ambiguity.

Options:

-o:<file> Output file.
